Position Summary

About the Role

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Supply Chain and Logistics Operations Intern to support daily supply chain activities, inventory management, transportation coordination, and operational improvement initiatives. This role provides hands-on experience in logistics planning, procurement support, inventory control, data analysis, and cross-functional collaboration within a growing logistics firm in Cameroon's South West Region.

What You'll Do

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor inventory levels and maintain accurate inventory records across all product lines.
  • Support inbound and outbound logistics operations, including shipment tracking and delivery coordination.
  • Build and analyze supply chain data to identify trends, bottlenecks, and improvement opportunities.
  • Coordinate with suppliers, carriers, warehouses, and internal departments to ensure smooth operations.
  • Prepare periodic reports on inventory performance, transportation costs, and key service metrics.
  • Assist in demand forecasting and supply planning activities to reduce waste and stockouts.
  • Support procurement teams with purchase order tracking and supplier communications.
  • Participate in process improvement projects aimed at increasing efficiency and reducing operational costs.
  • Ensure compliance with company policies, safety standards, and regulatory requirements.
  • Maintain accurate documentation and records related to logistics and supply chain activities.
What We're Looking For

Qualifications

Required
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Operations Management, Business Administration, Industrial Engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Access, and other Office applications.
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines under pressure.
Preferred
  • Knowledge of supply chain concepts: inventory management, transportation, procurement, and demand planning.
  • Experience with ERP systems such as SAP or Oracle ERP.
  • Familiarity with data analysis and reporting tools.
  • Previous internship, project, or coursework related to supply chain or logistics operations.
