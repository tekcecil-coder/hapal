Who We Are

Built on
Trust &
Reliability

Havilah Partners Logistics (HAPAL) is a diversified logistics and supply company proudly based in Kumba, South West Region. We were founded with one goal: to bridge the gap between quality products and the people who need them most.

From our fleet of rental vehicles and professional driver services to our extensive wholesale network of cleaning and hygiene products, HAPAL is a name synonymous with dependability, value, and regional expertise.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable logistics and product supply to every business and household in the region.

Our Reach

Operating from Kumba with supply chains extending across the South West and beyond.

Quality First

All products and services meet strict quality standards. We never compromise on what reaches our clients.

Partnership

We build long-term relationships. Your growth is our growth — we invest in your success.
